About IgniteCS

What is IgniteCS?

IgniteCS is an after school program that aims to expose high school students to Computer Science in a fun and friendly environment

Learn Computer Science

Develop reasoning and logic skills used in the field of Computer Science. Build mobile applications and get your ideas published in the Google Play Store.

Industry Seasoned Instructors

Learn from students and faculty of the Cal Poly Pomona Computer Science Department. Instructors have stories and lessons from industry to share!

Beginner Friendly

No prior coding experience? No Problem! We start with the basics and welcome students of all skill levels.

Free

IgniteCS is completely free for student participants. All equipment and instruction is provided free of charge.

Time & Location

IgniteCS instructors host courses at your local high school. Classes are held on Fridays from 3:30-5:30pm.
